About TechNest

Your trusted technology partner in Bangladesh

Our Story

TechNest was founded with a simple mission: to make genuine, high-quality technology accessible to everyone in Bangladesh. We noticed that too many customers were getting counterfeit products or paying inflated prices for basic PC components.

We started as a small team of tech enthusiasts who believed that every gamer, professional, and student deserved access to authentic products at fair prices. Today, TechNest has grown into one of Bangladesh's most trusted online destinations for computer components, gaming peripherals, and IT essentials.

Our commitment remains the same: every product we sell is 100% genuine, backed by manufacturer warranty, and delivered with care to your doorstep — anywhere in Bangladesh.
